Teacher Resources

  • Workshop Slides [pptx] - The slide deck we use in our PD workshops, in PowerPoint format. This includes the background and context slides, as well as all slides used during the professional development workshops.

  • Support Forums [Announcements | Discussion] - Want to be kept up-to-date about Bootstrap events, workshops, and curricular changes? Want to ask a question or pose a lesson idea for other Bootstrap teachers? These forums are the place to do it.

  • Workbook Solutions [pdf] - Completed exercises for the entire Student Workbook.

  • "What's Going on in this Graph?" [nytimes.com] - Once a month, the NYTimes posts an interesting data visualization. This is a great resource to spark the curiosity of young data scientists, and make for a perfect Notice & and Wonder!

  • "The Pudding" [pudding.cool] - Terrific deep dives into data analysis for interesting topics. Another wonderful resource for students, with great animations showing the transformation of how data is visualized as the research questions evolve.
